ABOUT US



"Like a Fantasy that feels Unreal".


London Bakers and Sweets is a European concept bakery founded in 2022 to bring the authentic taste of European home baking to Karachi.

In a span of two years, London Bakers and Sweets has opened in the city. Our cakes, cupcakes and brownies owe their unique look and exceptional flavor to top quality raw ingredients, our baking expertise, and a passionate team of people who love to live their lives by the oven.

At London Bakers we serve you with premium quality baked goods made with love using carefully sourced ingredients, blended in our special recipes that keep us just ‘That’ step ahead of the rest.

We know our customers are deeply concerned about what goes into their food so we work hard to ensure that every batch is baked from scratch and is preservative- and additive-free as far as possible.
